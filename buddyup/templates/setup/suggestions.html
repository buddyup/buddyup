A Few Suggestions

  • If you don't use your pdx email account very much, be sure to set up email forwarding to your main account so you don't miss messages from potential buddies!
  • Before creating a new study group be sure to make sure none of the other groups will work for you.
  • Out of respect for others, please do your best to show up to the groups you've RSVP'd to.
  • ...and don't be afraid to advertise your group during class.
